Welcome to Ngao Ya Imani International

We are committed to transforming lives in Laikipia Central, Kenya, by providing access to education, promoting environmental sustainability, empowering women, and supporting sustainable livelihoods. Our programs are designed to uplift and empower children and families, helping them overcome poverty and build brighter futures. Join us in creating lasting change and bringing hope to those in need, making a meaningful impact one community at a time. Together, we can foster resilience, opportunity, and growth for generations to come.

About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to follow Jesus Christ where the need is greatest, work with the poor and oppressed to promote human transformation, help make their voices heard, and empower them to challenge the structures that keep them in poverty.

Our Vision

Our vision is to see people freed from poverty, living transformed lives, and fulfilling their God-given potential. We pursue this vision through four key pillars: Education, Trainings, Skills & Empowerment, Women Empowerment, and Natural Resource Management.

Our Values

As a Christian organization, our work is inspired by Christian values, but we serve people of all faiths and backgrounds without discrimination. We strive to be Christ-centered, compassionate, courageous, truthful, and servant-hearted in all we do.

Our Programs

Education Pillar

We empower vulnerable children by providing access to safe, quality education from pre-primary to secondary levels. Through child sponsorship, we support education, healthcare, and essential needs. Additionally, we promote digital literacy to prepare children for the modern world.

  • Learn More

    • Trainings, Skills and Empowerment Pillar

    This pillar focuses on empowering youth through Ready to Work initiatives in collaboration with vocational facing industry-relevant certifications and hands-on training. We also provide financial literacy and entrepreneurship programs to equip individuals with essential financial management and business skills.

  • Learn More

    • Women Empowerment Pillar

    We emphasize empowering women who are often financially excluded. Through saving groups, women in rural areas can save, lend, and invest together, fostering dignity, household resilience, and social cohesion. Additionally, we train women on nutrition-sensitive agriculture, enabling them to provide basic needs and build livelihoods, breaking cycles of poverty.

  • Learn More

    • Environment Conservation and Integrated Livelihoods Pillar

    To combat climate change, we commit to projects that create green jobs and build resilience in vulnerable communities. Our campaigns target young people and children to promote awareness of climate issues. Together, we work to mitigate the impacts of climate change and foster sustainability.

  • Learn More

    • Donations

    Your donation to Ngao Ya Imani International provides nutritious meals, educational support, health resources, and empowerment programs for marginalized communities in Kenya. Contributions also aid families facing crises, helping them recover and rebuild. Join us in creating lasting change by donating once, supporting monthly, sponsoring a child, or legacy giving.
