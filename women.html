Women Empowerment Pillar

Empowering Women for Sustainable Development

This pillar addresses financial inclusion, leadership, and agricultural skills for women in marginalized communities. We aim to uplift women by providing resources, financial services, and access to market-oriented opportunities.

Key Areas of Focus

  • Savings Groups: Women form groups to pool savings and access loans, empowering them to start small businesses.
  • Nutrition-Sensitive Agriculture: Training in sustainable farming methods helps women improve food security and family health.
  • Leadership Development: Programs help women become leaders in their communities, fostering social and economic resilience.
