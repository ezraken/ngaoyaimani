Environment Conservation and Integrated Livelihoods Pillar

Fostering Environmental Resilience

This pillar promotes sustainable practices to combat climate change and preserve natural resources. We create green job opportunities and educate youth about environmental conservation.

Key Areas of Focus

  • Climate Resilience Projects: Initiatives like reforestation, water conservation, and sustainable agriculture.
  • Green Jobs and Livelihoods: We train communities in eco-friendly practices, including composting and organic farming.
  • Youth Engagement: Programs educate young people on climate issues, empowering them to lead change.
