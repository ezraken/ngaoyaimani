Donations

Your generous support helps us provide nutritious meals, educational sponsorships, and critical resources for children and families in marginalized communities across Kenya. Every donation, no matter the size, brings us closer to a future where children can grow up healthy, educated, and empowered to reach their full potential.

Where Your Donations Go

  • Nutritious Meals: Your support helps us provide daily meals and take-home rations for school children, helping combat malnutrition and improve their ability to focus and learn.
  • Educational Sponsorships: Donations go towards school fees, uniforms, books, and other essentials, enabling vulnerable children to access quality education and break the cycle of poverty.
  • Health and Sanitation Resources: We invest in clean water, sanitation facilities, and hygiene education to ensure children and families live in safe, healthy environments.
  • Empowerment Programs: Funds support skills training and empowerment initiatives that help families and young people gain sustainable livelihoods.
  • Emergency Relief: Contributions also provide critical support for families facing unexpected challenges, such as droughts or health crises, helping them recover and rebuild.

How You Can Make a Difference

When you give to Ngao Ya Imani International, you’re not just donating; you’re investing in the future of Kenya’s most vulnerable communities. Here are a few ways to contribute:

  • One-Time Donation: A single donation can make an immediate impact by helping fund our current programs and respond to urgent needs.
  • Monthly Support: By becoming a monthly supporter, you help us plan and expand our projects with reliable funding, reaching more children and families.
  • Sponsor a Child: Your sponsorship can provide ongoing support for a child’s education, nutrition, and well-being. Sponsorship gives a child the tools they need to succeed and brings lasting change.
  • Legacy Giving: Consider leaving a lasting legacy through a planned gift in your will. Legacy donations help us continue our work for years to come.

Make a Donation

Thank you for considering a donation to Ngao Ya Imani International. With your help, we can continue to transform lives, support sustainable growth, and offer hope to those who need it most. Together, we are building a brighter future for Kenya.
